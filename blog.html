What I Built: TallyAI

TallyAI is a Google Sheets template that categorizes expenses from plain English descriptions using AI built directly into the sheet.

Instead of opening a spreadsheet, finding the right category, typing the amount, selecting the date, and tagging it manually — you just type what you spent:

"client lunch at cafe 45"
"adobe subscription 55 monthly"
"freelance payment 1200 from acme corp"

The AI extracts the date, amount, and category automatically.

How The AI Works

No ChatGPT. No API key. No subscription. The AI runs entirely within Google Sheets.

You type: "uber to airport 35"
→ AI detects: amount ($35), date (today), type (transportation)
→ Auto-fills: Date, Amount, and Category columns
→ AI remembers corrections for next time

Your data stays in your Google account. Nothing is sent to external servers.
