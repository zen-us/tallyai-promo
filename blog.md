## What I Built: TallyAI

TallyAI is a Google Sheets template that **categorizes expenses from plain English descriptions** using AI built directly into the sheet.

Instead of:
- Opening a spreadsheet
- Finding the right category column
- Typing in the amount
- Selecting the date
- Tagging it manually

You just **type what you spent**:

> "client lunch at cafe 45"
> "adobe subscription 55 monthly"
> "freelance payment 1200 from acme corp"

## How The AI Works

The key insight: **you don't need ChatGPT or any external AI service.** The AI runs entirely within the Google Sheet using built-in functions.

The flow:

```
You type: "uber to airport 35"
        ↓
AI detects: amount ($35), date (today), type (transportation)
        ↓
Auto-fills: Date column, Amount column, Category column
        ↓
You review → correct if needed → AI remembers for next time
```

No API keys. No subscriptions. No data sent to third parties. Everything stays in your Google account.

## Features That Actually Matter

### 1. CSV Bank Import
Drop your bank's CSV export into the sheet. The AI categorizes your entire transaction history in one shot. No manual matching.

### 2. Multi-Currency
Work with clients in USD, EUR, GBP, JPY — any currency. Live exchange rates via built-in Google Finance functions.

### 3. Monthly Narrative Reports
Instead of a wall of numbers, the AI writes a plain-English summary:
> "You spent $450 more on software subscriptions this month (+18% vs last month). Client revenue was $2,800 from 4 projects. Your effective tax rate is approximately 22%."

### 4. 7 Languages
English, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese. Describe expenses in whatever language is natural to you.

### 5. Voice Dictation
On your phone? Open Google Sheets, tap the mic, say "client invoice 1500." The AI handles categorization. Hands-free bookkeeping.
