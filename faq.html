Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TallyAI AI Bookkeeping

What is TallyAI?

TallyAI is a Google Sheets template that uses AI to auto-categorize expenses from plain English descriptions. Instead of manually filling category columns, you just type what you spent — the AI extracts the date, amount, and category automatically.

Do I need to know Google Sheets?

Basic familiarity is enough. If you can open a spreadsheet and type text, you can use TallyAI. The AI handles all the complex parts — categorization, calculations, and reports.

Do I need a ChatGPT or OpenAI API key?

No. The AI runs entirely within Google Sheets using built-in pattern matching and Google Apps Script. No external API keys, no ChatGPT account, no subscriptions. Your data never leaves your Google account.

Is my financial data safe?

Yes. Everything stays in your Google account. TallyAI does not send your data to any external servers, third parties, or cloud AI services. The AI runs locally within your spreadsheet. Compare this to bookkeeping apps that store all your financial data on their servers.

Is this a one-time purchase?

Yes. You pay once and own the template forever. You can make unlimited copies to your own Google Drive. There are no monthly fees, no subscription tiers, no hidden charges. The free edition on GitHub is also available forever at no cost.

How does the AI categorization work?

When you type an expense description like "coffee 4.50", the AI:

  • Extracts the amount ($4.50)
  • Detects the date (today or a mentioned date)
  • Matches keywords to the correct category (Food & Drink)
  • Fills all three columns automatically

If the AI gets a category wrong, you can correct it — the AI remembers for next time. This auto-learning means the template gets smarter the more you use it.

Can I use TallyAI on my phone?

Yes. TallyAI works in the Google Sheets mobile app on iOS and Android. You can type expenses directly, or use your phone's voice dictation — tap the microphone on your keyboard and say "client invoice 1500" for hands-free bookkeeping.

Which languages are supported?

The Pro edition supports 7 languages: English, Chinese (中文), Spanish (Español), French (Français), German (Deutsch), Portuguese (Português), and Japanese (日本語). The AI understands category keywords in each language.

How do I import bank statements?

In the Pro edition, you can upload your bank's CSV export directly. The AI categorizes your entire transaction history automatically. Most bank and credit card CSVs are supported. The AI flags any uncertain transactions for your review.

What if I need a custom category?

You can add custom categories directly in the Categories sheet. The AI automatically adapts and starts recognizing expenses for your new categories. This makes TallyAI flexible for any business type.

Do I need internet access?

Yes, TallyAI requires internet access to run in Google Sheets. However, if you enable Google Drive offline mode, you can view your data without internet. AI categorization requires internet to process descriptions.

Which professions have specific templates?

TallyAI has 25+ profession-specific versions including: photographers, graphic designers, personal trainers, salon owners, real estate agents, Etsy sellers, landlords, wedding planners, consultants, freelance writers, web developers, musicians, social media managers, virtual assistants, dog walkers, house cleaners, handymen, bakers, caterers, yoga instructors, restaurant owners, and more.

Can I share the template with my accountant?

Yes. Since the template lives in Google Sheets, you can share it like any other Google Doc. Your accountant can view or edit the data. The expense reports and monthly summaries are easy to export or print.

Is there a refund policy?

TallyAI is a Google Sheets template, not a full accounting app. It's lighter, cheaper (one-time $12.99 vs $15+/month), and gives you full control over your data.
